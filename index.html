Our Story

Where Nature
Meets the Cup

Nisarg was founded on a simple belief that good food should come from good places. We source our coffee directly from the lush estates of Coorg, Araku Valley and Wayanad, roasting in small batches every 36 hours to preserve the terroir in every sip.

Our kitchen runs on seasonal, locally-grown produce. No artificial flavours, no shortcuts. Just honest ingredients treated with skill, care and a deep respect for the land they come from.
